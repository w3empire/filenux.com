What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chatbot developed by OpenAI and launched in November 2022. It’s based on a large language model, which allows it to:

  • Have human-like conversations: You can chat with ChatGPT on a variety of topics, and it will respond in a way that feels natural and engaging.
  • Generate different creative text formats: It can write poems, code, scripts, musical pieces, email, letters, etc., based on your instructions.
  • Answer your questions in an informative way: It can access and process information from the real world through Google Search and keep its response consistent with search results.

However, ChatGPT has some limitations:

  • Free version has limitations: While there’s a free tier, it has limitations on query length and frequency.
  • May not always be factually accurate: Its responses are based on the information it has been trained on, which may not always be accurate or up-to-date.
  • Can be biased: Like any large language model, it can reflect biases present in its training data.

‚Ñπ ChatGPT is a powerful tool for engaging in conversations and generating creative text formats, but it’s important to be aware of its limitations and use it responsibly.
