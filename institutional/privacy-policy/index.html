FileNUX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2024

Introduction:

FileNUX (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects the privacy of our users (“you” or “your”). Your use of the Services constitutes your agreement to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect:

We collect two types of information from users:

1. Personal Information:

This information allows us to identify you as an individual. It may include:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • User-generated content (comments, reviews, feedback)
  • Usage data (IP address, browsing history, device information)
  • Account information (username, password)

2. Non-Personal Information:

This information does not identify you as an individual. It may include:

  • Aggregated usage data
  • Device information
  • Analytics data

How We Collect Information:

We collect information through various methods, including:

  • User input (contact forms, comments, reviews)
  • Automatic data collection (cookies, web analytics)
  • Third-party integrations

How We Use Information:

We use information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Services
  • To personalize your experience
  • To communicate with you
  • To analyze usage data
  • To comply with legal obligations

Disclosure of Information:

We may disclose information to third parties under the following circumstances:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating the Services
  • Law enforcement agencies or other legal entities as required by law
  • If we believe there is a threat to harm you or others

Data Retention:

We retain personal information for as long as it is necessary to provide the Services or comply with legal obligations. We may also retain anonymized aggregate data for research purposes.

Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Choices:

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. You can also control cookie settings and opt out of certain data collection practices.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on the Services.
