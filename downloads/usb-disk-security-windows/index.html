USB Disk Security is a software program developed by Zbshareware that provides protection to your computer from viruses, malware, and other harmful threats that may come from external storage devices such as USB flash drives, external hard drives, and memory cards. The software is easy to install and use. Once installed, it will run in the background, constantly monitoring your computer for any threats that may come from external storage devices. Whenever you connect a USB flash drive or other external storage device to your computer, USB Disk Security will scan it for any potential threats. If any threats are detected, it will alert you and provide you with the option to either quarantine or remove the infected files.

With USB Disk Security, users have the ability to automatically immunize their USB flash drive against any potential threats. This means that whenever you connect your USB flash drive to any other computer, it will be protected against any viruses, malware, or other harmful threats that may be present on that computer.

Another useful feature of USB Disk Security is its ability to password-protect your USB flash drive. This means that even if your USB flash drive falls into the wrong hands, the person who finds it will not be able to access any of the files on it without entering the correct password. This is a great way to protect your sensitive files and information from unauthorized access.

USB Disk Security also comes with a built-in task manager that allows you to monitor the processes running on your computer. This can be useful for identifying any suspicious processes that may be running in the background and potentially harming your computer.
